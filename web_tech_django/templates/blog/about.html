About Kigali Tours

Kigali Tours is a tour operator in Kigali, Nyarutarama which organizes and conducts individual tours and excursions in Kigali and Central Africa since 2023.

We help to travel safely and comfortably, away from noisy and large groups of tourists, because only in this way you can feel the beauty of the world and stay alone with nature.

The main mode of transport in our company is full-drive off-road vehicles. Jeeps will take our customers to the most remote places in Kigali, Kampala and Nairobi. Time saving, comfort and clear pictures while driving are only a part of what this mode of transport can boast. When travelling long distances, rest on the road is very important.

  • Are you planning to visit Kigali or Central Africa?
  • Have you already bought tickets to Gisenyi or Rusizi or any other area around central Africa and are looking for a tour?
  • Are you already in Kigali or any region in EastAfrica and want to go on a trip the next day?
  • A tour bought with another company cancelled and you want to start on a trip as soon as possible?

In all these cases, please contact us and we will definitely help you. Our consultants answer within 2 hours and are available 24 hours 7 days a week.
